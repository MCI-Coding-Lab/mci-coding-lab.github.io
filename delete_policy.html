How to Delete Your Account and Data - Carless

If you wish to delete your Carless account and all associated data, you can follow the steps outlined below. Please note that once your account is deleted, all information associated with it will be permanently removed from our servers.

Steps to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Carless app on your device.
  2. Log in to your account using your username and password.
  3. Once logged in, tap the Settings button located in the top right corner of the screen, marked as a specific icon.
  4. Tap the Delete Account button.
  5. Once confirmed, your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted from our servers.

What Data is Deleted?

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your username and password
  • All car information and documents you have uploaded

No data is retained after your account is deleted. We do not keep any backup or copies of your information.

Important Notes

  • There is no direct way to delete individual pieces of data; the only option is to delete your entire account.
  • Your data will not be deleted without your explicit consent.
