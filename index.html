Privacy Policy for Carless

Effective Date: 2024-08-21

1. Introduction

Welcome to Carless. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting the information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information when using the Carless mobile app.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information:

When you create an account on Carless, we collect only the following information:

  • Username
  • Password

No other personal information is required or collected during the account creation process.

Car and Document Storage:

Carless allows you to store information related to car plates or names and upload documents associated with those cars. These documents may contain sensitive data related to the car owners.

3. How We Use Your Information

We do not sell, share, or otherwise use your information for any purpose beyond providing you with the core functionality of the Carless app. Specifically:

  • Account Information: Your username and password are used solely to manage your account and enable you to log in to the app.
  • Car Information and Documents: The car information and any documents you upload are stored securely and are accessible only to you within the app.

4. Data Storage and Security

We take the security of your data seriously. Carless implements industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including but not limited to encryption and secure data storage. However, please be aware that no method of electronic storage or transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share, sell, or disclose your personal information, car information, or uploaded documents to third parties. Your data is yours alone, and we respect your privacy.

6. Your Rights

As a user of Carless, you have the following rights concerning your data:

  • Access: You have the right to access the information stored in your account at any time.
  • Modification: You can modify your car information and uploaded documents as needed.
  • Deletion: You can delete your account, car information, and documents at any time. Once deleted, this data is permanently removed from our servers.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. When we make changes, we will update the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or the data we collect, please contact us at mihai.ionescu@mcicoding.ro.
